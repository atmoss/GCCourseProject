Tidy data from large raw data from activity monitoring with cell phones.

Variables present in mytidydata.md :

Subjects : Number (integer) that indicates the volunteer number (a person) who perform the data collection with his cell phone.
Activities: Description (char) of the activity perform by volunteer. Values are "LAYING", "SITTING", "STANDING", "WALKING", "WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS" and "WALKING_UPSTAIRS".

The next are the variables names. Each of them is the mean calculated by Subject and Activity. This values are normalized and bounded within [-1,1]
